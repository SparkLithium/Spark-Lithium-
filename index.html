Who We Are

Safe disposal.
Real impact.

Spark Lithium is a nonprofit organization on a mission to eliminate the hidden hazards of improperly discarded vapes and lithium batteries. These small devices pack a punch — lithium cells can spontaneously combust when crushed, punctured, or exposed to heat inside a garbage truck or sorting facility.

We partner with municipalities, retailers, and schools to place free, clearly marked disposal boxes that make doing the right thing effortless — for everyone, right here in Charlotte and across North Carolina.

The Process

How it works

01
Find a Drop-Off Box

Locate a Spark Lithium disposal box at a partner retailer, school, or community center near you across Charlotte and the Carolinas.

02
Drop In Your Device

Deposit your used vape, e-cigarette, or dead lithium battery — no registration required. Just drop and go. Simple as that.

03
We Handle the Rest

Boxes are collected and transported to certified e-waste processors who safely reclaim materials and neutralize battery hazards.

04
Safer Communities

Every device properly disposed of is one fewer potential fire. Your action protects workers, neighbors, and the environment we all call home.

Why It Matters

The risks are real.
The solution is simple.

🔥
Facility Fires

Lithium batteries in trash compactors and sorting machines are a leading cause of devastating fires at recycling and waste facilities across North America.

👷
Worker Safety

Sanitation workers are on the front lines. Exploding batteries cause injuries every year — injuries that are entirely preventable with proper disposal habits.

🌍
Environmental Harm

Vapes contain nicotine, heavy metals, and plastics that leach into soil and groundwater when landfilled. Proper recycling keeps these chemicals out of our earth.
